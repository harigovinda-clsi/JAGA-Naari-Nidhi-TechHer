About Naari Nidhi

Empowering women through financial independence since 2020

Naari Nidhi (which translates to "Women's Treasure") was founded with a singular mission: to bridge the financial inclusion gap for women in developing economies. We believe that when women have control over their finances, they transform not only their own lives but also their families and communities.

Our journey began in rural India, where we witnessed firsthand the challenges women face in accessing basic financial services. Traditional banking systems often overlook women due to lack of formal identification, limited financial literacy, and restrictive social norms.

Today, we serve over 500,000 women across 12 countries, providing innovative financial solutions that work within their unique constraints and cultural contexts.

Our Mission

To provide every woman with the tools, knowledge, and resources she needs to achieve financial independence, regardless of her location, education level, or social status.

Our Vision

A world where women have equal access to financial services and opportunities, enabling them to become drivers of economic growth and social change in their communities.

Our Core Values

Women-Centered Design

All our solutions are designed with women's unique needs, constraints, and aspirations at the forefront.

Community First

We believe in the power of women's collectives and build tools that strengthen community bonds.

Accessibility

Our solutions work on any device, in any language, with or without internet connectivity.

Security & Privacy

We prioritize the safety and confidentiality of women's financial data above all else.
